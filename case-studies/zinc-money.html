Case study · Internship

Zinc Money

Community growth, product GTM, and AI-powered sales analysis for a fintech company serving Indian students pursuing education abroad.

4,000+ community members
15-20% engagement rate
60% faster pitch optimization

Background

I was recruited at 18 as intern number one at a new multi-million-dollar fintech company by the former head of FamPay, one of India's leading neobanks, after he read my content on LinkedIn and reached out directly.

I joined Zinc Money to own the community growth and engagement strategy for Indian students pursuing education abroad.

Challenge

Build a community from zero in a fragmented market where trust and user retention were critical to product adoption and monetization.

Research & Strategy

I conducted market research to determine whether a multi-platform community structure using WhatsApp and Discord would optimize engagement and retention.

Based on my findings, I chose a dual-platform approach to capture both synchronous engagement through Discord live events and asynchronous engagement through WhatsApp groups.

Execution

I launched and scaled the community to 4,000+ members across both platforms, achieving a consistent 15-20% engagement rate for live events and day-to-day activity.

I partnered with study abroad subject matter experts Singh in the USA, Avanti Nagral, and Ishan Sharma, creators with millions of combined followers and tens of millions of views across the internet. Their study-abroad-focused content attracted high-intent users and accelerated community growth.

I developed content strategies that increased engagement and executed organic marketing campaigns that drove reach and retention. I also delivered 30+ free college counseling sessions, establishing myself as a trusted advisor and building direct relationships with core users.

Product & Operations

I led the community launch and GTM of our proprietary AI college consulting service before the AI hype cycle, turning it into the leading source of product users for the service.

I helped build the team by referring talent and getting one additional intern hired, scaling the founding team to three members.

Data & Optimization

I implemented ChatGPT-powered analysis on 500+ recorded sales calls to extract insights on how to pitch financial services more effectively to decision makers.

This reduced pitch refinement time by 60% and improved conversion messaging through data-driven positioning.

Impact

4,000+ community members
15-20% engagement rate
30+ free counseling sessions
500+ sales calls analyzed
60% faster pitch optimization
#1 product acquisition channel

Key Learnings

I learned how to scale communities without paid acquisition through authentic partnerships and direct engagement.

Trust-building through free counseling sessions generated stronger retention signals than vanity metrics. I also learned that AI-powered analytics on customer conversations can rapidly compress the feedback loop and improve go-to-market messaging.
