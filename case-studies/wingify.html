Background & Recruitment

I was directly recruited by my PE MD mentor from Everstone Capital to support financial integration at Wingify during a cross-border merger.

Deal & Scope

I gained hands-on exposure to post-acquisition integration, applying financial modeling, accounting standards, and systems thinking to live workstreams spanning revenue operations, treasury, and financial reporting across multiple geographies and entities.

Financial Modeling & Analytical Work

I supported my manager with comprehensive analytical and modeling work across multiple functional areas. I built Excel analyses to model intercompany eliminations and consolidation rules across geographically dispersed entities, stress testing various consolidation scenarios under different FX and regulatory regimes.

I developed financial projections supporting integration sequencing decisions and created models to support treasury optimization across multiple jurisdictions. I also built fair value adjustment models for employee benefit accounting under Indian accounting standards, computing implicit interest components and amortization schedules.

This analytical foundation underpinned downstream workstream planning and served as a primary input for senior stakeholder discussions on integration timing and resource allocation.

Technical Accounting & Multi-Standard Analysis

I analyzed complex intercompany transactions requiring understanding across multiple Indian Accounting Standards covering financial instruments recognition, fair value measurement, related party disclosures, consolidation mechanics, provisions, and foreign exchange effects.

I synthesized how single transactions flow through multiple accounting frameworks, translating valuation decisions into consolidation treatment and disclosure requirements.

Financial Consolidation Platform

I was involved in the implementation of a global financial consolidation platform across multiple entities and geographies with a defined go-live timeline.

I attended sessions to understand how the system would consolidate financial data across disparate entities while handling multiple currencies and regulatory regimes. I studied intercompany eliminations, consolidation rules, and how the system would feed into financial reporting.

I also worked on understanding data migration requirements and system setup to support accurate consolidation.

Treasury & Cross-Border Operations

I engaged with cross-border treasury operations involving foreign exchange and central bank compliance, learning how to structure cash flows and treasury operations across geographies while adhering to local regulatory requirements.

I worked on control implementation, including segregation of duties and fraud prevention. I was also exposed to banking operations, account structures, and how post-acquisition entities consolidate banking relationships.

Systems Integration & Revenue Operations

I was exposed to the integration of critical commercial and financial systems, understanding data flows from customer order through billing to financial recognition.

I worked on designing controls architecture, including approval workflows, master data validation, and compliance screening in operational processes.

Process Automation

I engaged in discussions on automating financial consolidation workflows, exploring how to reduce manual effort in closing cycles and improve reconciliation efficiency.

Cross-Functional Exposure

I worked alongside PE sponsors, leading advisory firms, and senior internal stakeholders, gaining a ground-up understanding of how large-scale integrations are structured and executed.

I regularly synthesized inputs across functions to support decision-making while shadowing subject matter experts across audit, valuation, and compliance.

Impact & Learnings

This experience taught me how strategic decisions made at the deal level translate into technical accounting treatment and enterprise systems architecture.

I developed fluency in cross-border compliance, multi-standard accounting, and the operational complexity of consolidating disparate financial and operational systems.

I learned that successful integrations require both financial modeling rigor and the ability to synthesize complex accounting questions across multiple standards simultaneously.
