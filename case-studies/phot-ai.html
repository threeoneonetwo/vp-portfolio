Background & Recruitment

I was recruited in under two hours by the founder of a marketing agency contracted by the company after he reviewed my work. In July, I was poached by the founder of Phot.ai to join the company directly as an in-house growth lead.

Challenge

Scale a Gen AI startup's brand from zero in a saturated market while building sustainable creator and content systems that could support high-velocity growth.

Content Research & Playbook Development

I started by conducting deep market research across our target niches. I analyzed videos with 1M+ views to identify what content patterns actually drove engagement.

I documented video hooks, text messaging, and creative elements that resonated across these high-performing pieces. Based on these signals, I built Excel sheets mapping the exact hooks and creative formulas that worked.

This gave me a repeatable playbook instead of relying on guesswork. I then systematized the research into detailed content briefs that the team used to produce campaigns consistently.

Execution & Growth

Using this data-driven playbook, I scaled the brand from 0 to 45K+ followers and 5M+ views within one month.

Each piece of content was tested against the patterns we identified, optimized based on performance, and iterated on weekly. This process revealed which angles and messaging consistently won, which I fed back into the next cycle of content creation.

Creator Program & Partnerships

I built the U.S. UGC program by leveraging my existing networks in the USA to partner with a UGC agency for free.

Rather than sourcing creators individually, I replicated our content playbook for the UGC agency, teaching them the same video hook and creative patterns that were working for our owned content. This meant UGC creators produced content aligned with our winning formula while maintaining their authentic voice.

Impact & Learnings

This taught me that systematic content research beats creative intuition. By reverse-engineering what works at scale and documenting the formula, we eliminated guesswork and built a repeatable system.

Leveraging networks and partnerships to access creator pools at no cost accelerated growth. The key insight was that the playbook itself became the asset, not just individual pieces of content.
